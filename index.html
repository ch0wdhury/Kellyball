Why choose me?

Researching and booking the perfect trip whilst making sure everyone is happy and catered for can be a challenge!

Your time away is precious and attention to detail can mean the difference between a good holiday and a fantastic holiday!

Having your very own “Travel PA” on hand every step of the way will keep you stress free, within budget and organised.

Time

I can save you loads of it & let’s face it, not many of us have that much going space. So, who wouldn’t want their own “Travel PA” working on creating the perfect holiday for them?!

Stress

Researching & booking the perfect getaway is time consuming & can be stressful, especially if there are a lot of you! I am on hand every step of the way, ensuring that everyone is catered for & nothing is forgotten so you can sit back & relax.

Money

Again, it’s very likely I will save you some! I want to dispel the myth that using a travel agent is more expensive - it’s not. I do not charge for my services, the travel companies I work with pay me so you pay the same as what you would if you had booked direct.

Tailor-made

Aside from having access to destinations all over the world at the best rates, I can book the perfect attractions for you, find the best places to eat/drink& even advise on the cheapest and quickest way to get around.
